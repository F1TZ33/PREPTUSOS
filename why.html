Why Preptus

Built to get honest answers without putting people on the defensive.

Traditional reviews often trigger guarded responses, partial truth, and late-stage insight. Preptus is designed to surface operating patterns early by assessing how the business is set up, not who is to blame.

Systems, not people

We assess role clarity, workflow, stock behaviour, supplier influence, and pressure points across the business. That keeps the process neutral and easier to engage with honestly.

Behaviour before numbers

Operational decline usually shows up in day-to-day patterns before it appears in reporting. Preptus uses those earlier signals to identify risk sooner.

Pattern-based outcomes

Rather than generic advice, the assessment maps findings to the most suitable level of engagement so the next step is proportionate and practical.

Methodology

What Preptus is looking for

Operational flow

How work moves, where it stalls, how clearly responsibilities are understood, and whether the branch feels controlled or reactive.

Inventory behaviour

Stock confidence, reactivity in ordering, hidden imbalance, and whether capital is being tied up in the wrong places.

Supplier dynamics

Supplier engagement, reliance, and whether purchasing behaviour is being shaped by commercial pressure rather than demand.

Growth pressure

Whether systems, staffing, and routines are keeping pace with workload — or quietly falling behind.

Why it converts

Free self-assessment first. Deeper engagement only if needed.

The diagnostic can stand alone as a useful self-assessment. If a client wants more clarity, they can save results, request a review, and then move into pricing for the recommended level.

Preptus is a structured diagnostic system designed to surface operational patterns early and recommend the right level of involvement.
