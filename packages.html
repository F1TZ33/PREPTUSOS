Packages

Six levels of engagement, recommended by the diagnostic.

The assessment is designed to identify the most suitable level of involvement. That keeps the first recommendation proportionate and tied to what the business actually needs.

Level 1

Foundation

Diagnostic-led clarity with a written summary of pressure points and immediate opportunities.

  • Best for businesses that are relatively stable but want an outside read before issues build.
Level 2

Insight

Broader interpretation of findings with stronger explanation around emerging risk and operational friction.

  • Best for businesses seeing warning signs but still functioning with reasonable control.
Level 3

Optimisation

A more active assessment designed to isolate bottlenecks, overlap, and hidden inefficiency.

  • Best for businesses feeling the strain of growth, complexity, or inconsistent execution.
Level 4

Intervention

Hands-on diagnostic engagement where operational strain is clearly affecting performance.

  • Best for businesses with duplicated effort, workflow breakdowns, or recurring service issues.
Level 5

Stabilisation

Focused support where the priority is regaining control, clarity, and commercial stability.

  • Best for businesses under sustained pressure or dealing with visible misalignment.
Level 6

Critical

Highest-engagement pathway for businesses where the current setup is materially impairing performance.

  • Best for businesses needing urgent clarity before decline becomes more expensive to reverse.
Separate offering

Pre-Acquisition Assessment

A distinct engagement for buyers, investors, or operators wanting a structured read on operational quality, hidden risk, inventory behaviour, and practical acquisition considerations before proceeding.

Preptus is a structured diagnostic system designed to surface operational patterns early and recommend the right level of involvement.
