A structured qualification method

Preptus OS™ uses a consistent assessment approach to interpret operating pressure and recommend a practical starting point. The detail behind that method stays proprietary, while the experience for visitors stays simple and useful.

What the method considers

The assessment looks at signals such as clarity, operating discipline, leadership visibility, execution consistency, and the amount of pressure currently present in the business.

What you receive

Rather than a raw score, visitors receive a recommended engagement level and a clearer next step. That keeps the experience practical, commercial, and easy to act on.
